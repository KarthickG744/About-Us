✦ Who We Are

Built by dreamers.
Refined by craft.

Luminary is a full-service creative studio founded in 2024, headquartered in San Francisco, CA. We partner with forward-thinking brands, startups, and cultural institutions to craft experiences that are both beautiful and enduring.

Our multidisciplinary team spans brand strategy, digital design, and creative direction. We believe the best work emerges from radical honesty, obsessive craft, and genuine curiosity about the world.

Every project begins with deep listening — we learn your world before we shape it, because great creative work is always rooted in truth.

Team Guidance & Instructions

🔒 Team Members Only
01
💻

Set Up Your Workstation

Before your first day, ensure your equipment is ready:

  • Install Figma, Slack, and Notion from the company portal
  • Request GitHub access from your team lead
  • Set up 2FA on all company accounts
  • Join the #welcome and #general Slack channels
02
📋

Complete Required Training

All new team members must complete the following within Week 1:

  • Company culture and values walkthrough (Notion)
  • Data privacy and security training (1 hr)
  • Brand standards and tone-of-voice guide
  • Tool access request via IT portal
03
🤝

Meet Your Team

Schedule 1:1 intro calls within your first week:

  • 30-min intro with your direct manager
  • 15-min intro with each team member in your dept.
  • Attend the weekly all-hands (every Monday 10 AM)
  • Join your department's Slack channel
04
🎯

First 30-Day Goals

Your manager will set these with you, but expect to:

  • Shadow at least 2 active client projects
  • Complete a small independent task or deliverable
  • Submit your 30-day check-in form via Notion
  • Identify areas where you need extra support
